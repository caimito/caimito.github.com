Developer Advocate & Enterprise Agility Consultant

Modern organizations face a dual challenge: delivering quality software reliably while aligning leadership, governance, and change initiatives. When these work separately, friction arises. Together, they drive transformation.

Enterprise Agility Consultant

Organizational & Program Level

  • Aligns executives, PMO, product, compliance
  • Designs hybrid governance (Agile, Lean, regulatory)
  • Supports change management across stakeholder groups
  • Shapes strategic initiatives and delivery structures

Developer Advocate

Engineering & Delivery Level

  • Works with teams through pairing/mobbing
  • Improves codebases, tests, architecture, CI/CD
  • Ensures safe delivery in small increments
  • Translates technical constraints for leadership

Why It Works

  • Leadership sees what teams can actually deliver
  • Teams gain conditions for stable, predictable delivery
  • Change initiatives grounded in reality
  • Regulatory constraints integrated without slowing flow

Result: No friction between "how we run projects" and "how we build software".

Key Benefits

  • Strategic alignment: Governance supports delivery
  • Faster value: Earlier feedback loops
  • Lower risk: Better practices, clearer decisions
  • Sustainable change: Capability persists after exit
  • Executive confidence: Coherent program + engineering guidance

How It Works

  1. Consultant establishes alignment, governance, stakeholder clarity
  2. Advocate ensures teams operate effectively within framework
  3. Regular touchpoints keep governance and engineering connected
  4. Technical feedback informs leadership decisions

Best For

  • Legacy-heavy systems
  • Regulated environments (financial services, compliance-heavy)
  • Multi-team programs
  • Product-centric transformation
  • Leadership aligned but teams struggling (or vice versa)

The Partnership: Different layers, no overlap, mutual reinforcement. When boundaries are clear and workstreams connected, organizations both decide well and deliver well.
